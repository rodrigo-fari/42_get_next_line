Naruto (??) is a series of manga written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto, which tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who constantly seeks recognition and dreams of becoming Hokage, the leader ninja of his village. The story is divided into two parts, the first part takes place in the years of Naruto's pre-adolescence, and the second part takes place in his teens. The series is based on two one-shot mangas of Kishimoto: Karakuri (1995), and that because of him Kishimoto won an honorable mention in Shueisha's Hop Step award Shueishathe following year, and Naruto (1997). The publisher Panini Comics licensed and published the manga in three versions in Brazil, and in Portugal is the publisher Devir Manga who licenses and publishes the manga.
